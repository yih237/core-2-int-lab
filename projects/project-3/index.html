The NYC Squirrel Census

About

The Squirrel Census is a multimedia science, design, and storytelling project focusing on the Eastern gray (Sciurus carolinensis). They count squirrels and present their findings to the public. Contents

The contents of this webpage include visualizations of the data provided by the volunteer squirrel sighters about squirrels. The different data include the fur color, the highlight fur color, the interaction of the squirrels with humans, and the activities that they are doing.

Instructions

You can see the squirrel sighting information in different locations throughout New York City and the general data of squirrels in New York City. This website is intended for squirrel lovers in NYC and anyone that is interested in these cute, flurry creatures!!
